ADTD: Attention Defecit Tower Defense

Warning: Playing this tower defense may result in feelings of being overwhelmed in awe and shock

How much information can you handle at once? Let's see...

Enemies

Demon, summons portals using it's left hand to let enemies float through time.

Angel, locks up towers and forces you to buy them again. Kill them quick or your forces will be diminished...

Distractor, forces you to play a minigame occasionally. Don't fail, or else everything suddenly will become much faster than you remember...

Necromancer, revives dead allies at a fraction of their true power. Kill quickly or face the consequences of your past murders

Tyrant, taunts all towers in a certain radius and forces them to target him. Plan to slow him down as much as you can, or you may not be able to kill every enemy in time...

Allies

Ice Tower, slows down nearby enemies for 5 seconds

Fire Tower, sets nearby enemies on fire for 5 seconds

Rage tower, makes all towers stronger for a short period of time

Bomb Tower, hits all enemies in a certain radius

Shop, allows you to buy items and towers. Don't get caught stealing...
